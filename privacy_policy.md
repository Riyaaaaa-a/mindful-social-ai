# Privacy Policy - Mindful Social

## Data Storage
All data collected by Mindful Social is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage. No data is transmitted to external servers except API calls to Hugging Face for AI responses.

## What Data We Collect
- Time spent on social media sites (Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, TikTok, Reddit)
- Scroll patterns and rates
- Mood logs (your mood entries)
- Custom goals and actions

## How We Use Data
- To provide accurate tracking statistics
- To generate personalized AI coaching messages
- To display mood insights and trends
- All processing is local to your device

## Data Sharing
We do NOT share your data with anyone. Your data never leaves your device except:
- API calls to Hugging Face (for AI text generation)
- These calls contain only your current request, not your history

## Data Deletion
You can delete all your data at any time through the extension settings.

## Contact
For privacy concerns, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.
